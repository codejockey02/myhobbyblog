Beholding a leaf  
Without uttering a word  
Bending the knee  
No, I'm not a bard  
Oh, why are you filled with ire ?  
I'm just a man  
With simple desires  
To behold your beauty   
Everywhere I go  
Will you help me ?  
Oh love, I bow.
